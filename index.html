Ways to Develop the Solomon Islands

1. Invest in Education & Skills Training

  • Improve access to quality education across all regions.
  • Support vocational training in agriculture, trades, tourism, and tech.
  • Empower youth through scholarships and mentorship.

2. Strengthen Agriculture & Local Industries

  • Support small farmers with tools and training.
  • Promote sustainable farming and fishing.
  • Encourage local businesses and traditional crafts.

3. Develop Sustainable Tourism

  • Promote eco-tourism and cultural heritage.
  • Train locals to run tourism services.
  • Market Solomon Islands as a unique travel destination.

4. Embrace Technology & Digital Access

  • Improve mobile and internet connectivity.
  • Teach digital skills and IT literacy.
  • Use online tools for education, health, and business.

5. Promote Good Governance & Anti-Corruption

  • Ensure transparency and community participation.
  • Strengthen local government services.
  • Involve youth and women in leadership roles.

6. Build Regional & International Partnerships

  • Cooperate with Pacific neighbors on shared goals.
  • Attract ethical foreign investment.
  • Work with NGOs and development partners.

7. Protect the Environment & Climate

  • Invest in climate resilience and adaptation.
  • Preserve forests, oceans, and biodiversity.
  • Use clean energy sources like solar and hydro.

8. Improve Health Services

  • Train local healthcare workers.
  • Expand access to clinics and essential care.
  • Bring healthcare to remote areas using mobile services.
